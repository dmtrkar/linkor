1
Introduction

Linkor is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Linkor mobile application.

2
Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Contact names, photos, and relationship data you manually enter
  • Notes, events, and dossier fields you create
  • Your display name entered during onboarding
  • Referral codes you enter or share

Information Collected Automatically:

  • App usage data and crash reports (via Apple's standard frameworks)
  • Purchase history for subscriptions and credit packs (processed by Apple)
  • Notification preferences

AI Features:

When you use AI Autofill, the names and details you search for are sent to Anthropic's API to generate dossier content. We do not store these queries on our servers. Quick Note text is sent to Anthropic's API for parsing. This data is not retained by us after processing.

3
How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Linkor app
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple's StoreKit
  • To send birthday and event reminders via local notifications
  • To generate AI-powered dossier content via Anthropic's API
  • To process referral rewards via CloudKit

4
Data Storage

All personal contact data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. We do not upload your contact data to our servers. If you enable iCloud sync, your data may be stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy terms.

5
Third-Party Services

Anthropic API: AI features use Anthropic's Claude API. Data sent includes search queries and note text. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data: anthropic.com/privacy

Apple Services: Purchases, notifications, and iCloud sync are handled by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/privacy

6
Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your contact data with anyone.

7
Data Retention and Deletion

All your data is stored on your device. You can delete all data by deleting the app. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

8
Children's Privacy

Linkor is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the app at any time
  • Export your data using the Export feature in Settings
  • Delete all data by removing the app
  • Opt out of notifications in your device Settings

10
Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the in-app support or by visiting the Support section in the App Store page for Linkor.

11
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
