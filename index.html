Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to the most common questions.
Linkor is a personal CRM app that helps you manage your relationships, build detailed dossiers on your contacts, and use AI to research and analyze your network — all stored privately on your device.
AI Autofill uses Anthropic's Claude with web search to find publicly available information about a person. You enter a name and company, and Linkor automatically populates the dossier with career history, social profiles, and more. It costs 10 credits per use.
Credits power AI features. You receive 20 free credits when you start. Additional credits can be purchased in packs (20, 75, 200, or 600). Credits never expire. You can buy more in Settings → Add Credits.
A subscription (Monthly or Annual) unlocks unlimited contacts beyond the free 3-contact limit. AI credits are purchased separately. The Lifetime plan includes unlimited contacts plus 100 bonus credits as a one-time purchase.
Yes. All your contact data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data. We do not upload your personal contact data to any server. AI features send only the specific search query or note to Anthropic's API and nothing else.
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Linkor, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of the billing period.
Open Linkor, go to Settings → Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Yes. Go to Settings → Export all data (JSON) to export a complete backup of all your contacts, dossier fields, and events as a JSON file. You can also export individual contact dossiers as PDF from the Full Dossier view.
The app interface is fully localized in English and Russian. AI responses automatically use your device language or you can set a preferred language in Settings → AI Response Language.
AI Autofill only finds publicly available information. If someone has a limited online presence, results may be sparse. Credits are not deducted if no results are found. Try adding the company name or city for better results.
